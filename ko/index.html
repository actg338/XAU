핵심 시스템

From signal to exit, full process management

Reduce emotional interference and transform trading rules into repeatable system processes.

01 / SIGNAL

SAR 신호 필터링

Identify the trend environment based on the parabolic indicator and support parameterized settings such as signal reversal.

02 / POSITION

지능형 진입 관리

Configure the master entry switch, minimum time interval, maximum position and slippage control.

03 / PROTECTION

동적 손절 보호

Adjust protection conditions as price changes, and support setting of trailing stop loss and buffer points.

04 / RISK

계좌 리스크 제한

Limit risk exposure through conditions such as balance, margin ratio, and dynamic position limit.

05 / EXIT

익절 청산 메커니즘

Supports single position target, protective take-profit and management rules after unrealized loss is triggered.

파라미터 설정

Adjust strategy parameters for different account environments and trading preferences to facilitate testing and iteration.
